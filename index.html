Glute A

Lengthened + shortened glute stimulus
Hip thrust
3 × 10 — 1RIR
Drive through heels, full lockout at top
Back extension (glute focused)
3 × 10 — 1RIR
Toes out, round lower back, feel the squeeze
Rest 2–3 min between sets. Wait at least 48hrs before Glute B.

Glute B

Hip dominant + unilateral glute
Hip thrust
3 × 10 — 1RIR
Go heavier than Glute A if possible
Cable step-up kickback (per leg)
3 × 10 — 1RIR
Step up controlled, squeeze fully before lowering
Rest 2–3 min. Don't do this the day after Glute A or Leg C.

Golden Rules
Progressive overload
Add weight when all sets feel easy at 1RIR — even 2.5kg counts.
Protein target
~85–108g/day (1.6–2g × 54kg) to support muscle growth.
Sets for growth
3–4 sets per exercise. 10–20 sets per muscle/week across 2 sessions.
Sets for strength
2–4 sets of 4–8 reps, heavy weight, 2–3 min rest.
